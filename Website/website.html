Recognizing the Silent Cry for Help

In today's digital age, many at-risk individuals may show warning signs online before seeking help. With millions of websites, articles, and forums available, those experiencing emotional distress might visit harmful content without others noticing.

Often, it's difficult for families, friends, or professionals to intervene until its too late. Suicide prevention has become more challenging because early signs are often overlooked.

Introducing Guardian: A Life-Saving Web Tracking System

Guardian is a cutting-edge suicide detection system that uses a Chrome extension to monitor the websites a user visits. With the help of artificial intelligence, Guardian analyzes the content of these sites to identify patterns of behavior linked to suicidal thoughts or tendencies.

If any potential signs of distress are detected, Guardian notifies trusted individuals so they can provide timely support and intervention.

Chrome Extension

The Guardian Extension analyses the browsing data to identify risk of suicide. The extension is lightweight and is supported on all chromium browsers.
The extension includes features to seek immediate help at your fingertips, including nearby psychiatrists based on location and real-time analytics.
